If You Have Questions,
These Are The 3 People You Need to Know.

Enrolling in a new school can be intimidating. It will be very likely that you'll have some questions along the way when it comes to enrolling with us. We want to make your transition into our family as easy as possible. Please don't hesitate to reach out!

Julia Mackey, Office Manager

Ms. Mackey is your main point of contact when it comes to all things registration and enrollment. She has been working in schools on behalf of D.C. kids for over a decade and in customer service roles for almost 30 years. She is your best resource.

Joey Webb, Principal

Mr. Webb is our Principal and has six years teaching experience before moving into school leadership. He has spent the last 4 years building Washington Leadership Academy into the school that it is today. Any questions about Academics can be directed to him.

Natalie Gould, Director of Operations

Ms. Gould has worked with a variety of organizations and education-focused nonprofits, to include the White House, Teach for America, and Democracy Prep. She is in charge of all logisitics and operational aspects of Washington Leadership Academy. If you have any logistical questions not related to registration and enrollment, please reach out to Ms. Gould.
